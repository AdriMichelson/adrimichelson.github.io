Posting old #TidyTuesdays! This one was looking at the price of white and red wine in different countires compared to the point value each wine got.
I ended up using ```gghighlight``` to highlight the wine. You can see the distribution and how flat both groups are, as in, price doesn't visually seem to correllate well with points especially when the lowest point value was 80 across the board. In addition, white wine's distribution of dots is a lot flatter, which means a number of cheaper white wines are just as good as the more expensive! As a white wine drinker, I approve!
