Posting old #TidyTuesdays! This one was looking at the price of white and red wine in different countires compared to the point value each wine got.
I ended up using ```gghighlight``` to highlight the wine. You can see the distribution and how flat both groups are, as in, price doesn't visually seem to correllate well with points especially when the lowest point value was 85 across the board.
